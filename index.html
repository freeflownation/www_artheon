OPEN ACCESS INITIATIVE

We support the international Open Access Initiative (initially, the Budapest Open Access Initiative, launched on February 14, 2002 in Budapest) providing unrestricted public access to researchers and authors work, much of which is publicly funded, allowing to reach larger audiences and increase interest, engagement and knowledge in art and culture.

Art is one of the most important tools to inspire faith and cultural growth giving a new perspective of looking at reality. We believe that FREE global access to fine art and culture is prerequisite to the development of human kind.

ARTHEON VR MUSEUM

is and always will be a free educational platform offering immersive, next level interaction with objects of cultural heritage, allowing deeper and more detailed perception and study and ensuring access to objects of art and culture for children, youth and all those who find it difficult or impossible to take a journey to art exhibitions.
